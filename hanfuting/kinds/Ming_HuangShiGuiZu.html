明朝·皇室贵族

明代皇帝、皇太子常服，戴乌纱折上巾，又称翼善冠，穿衮龙袍
前后及两肩绣有金盘龙纹样，交领或圆领，配玉带皮靴
此服用途较多。皇帝的常服上绣龙、翟纹及十二章纹
明代的龙形像较前代完善，它集中了各种动物的局部特征
在图案的构造和组织上也很有特色，除传统的行龙、云龙之外
还有团龙、正龙、坐龙、升龙、降龙等名目
皇后常礼服为戴龙凤珠翠冠、穿大衫、霞帔，内穿鞠衣
首服特髻上加龙凤饰，衣绣有织金龙凤纹
妃嫔、公主、郡主、王妃常礼服与皇后常礼服稍异,平日则穿袄裙

The emperors and crown princes of the Ming Dynasty wore regular clothes
wearing black gauze folded upper towels, also known as Yishan crowns and
wearing dragon robes.The front, back and shoulders are embroidered with the
pattern of gold pan dragons, cross-neck or round neck, and leather boots
with jade belt.This suit has many uses. The emperor's uniform is embroidered
with dragon Zhai and twelve chapter patterns.The image of the dragon
in the Ming Dynasty was more perfect than that of the previous generation
and it concentrated the local characteristics of various animals
The structure and organization of patterns are also very distinctive
except for the traditional Xinglong and Yunlong.There are also titles
such as Tuanlong, Zhenglong, Sitting Dragon, Rising Dragon, and
Subduing Dragon.The queen's regular dress is to wear a dragon
phoenix, pearl and emerald crown, a big shirt, a Xiapei, and a Juyi inside
The first dress is decorated with dragon and phoenix in a special button
and the clothes are embroidered with golden dragon and phoenix patterns
Concubines, princesses, princesses.and concubines are slightly different from
regular dresses for queens
