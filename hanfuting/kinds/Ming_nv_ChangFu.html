明朝·常服

明代上襦下裙的服装形式称为袄裙
比如立领、宽衣大袖紧袖口与大褶裙装等，都是大明服饰的特色
上襦除传统的交领外，到明中后期还出现立领
裙子除继承前代的百褶裙、褶裥裙外，还出现了马面裙
裙的颜色，初尚浅淡，虽有纹饰，但并不明显
至中期则多饰以膝襕，有刺绣、织金、烫金等形式的裙襕
崇祯初年，裙子多为素白，即使刺绣纹样
也仅在裙幅下边一、二寸部位缀以一条花边，作为压脚
”裙幅初为六幅，即所谓“裙拖六幅湘江水
后用八幅，腰间有很多细褶，行动辄如水纹
到了明末，裙子的装饰日益讲究，裙幅也增至十幅
腰间的褶裥越来越密
此时出现一种裙子，每褶都有一种颜色
”微风吹来，色如月华，故称“月华裙
腰间多挂上荷包、事件等物品，装饰与实用性兼备
The clothing form of upper skirt and lower skirt in Ming Dynasty is called aoqun
For example, stand-up collars, loose coats with large sleeves and tight cuffs, and large pleated
skirts are all characteristics of Ming Dynasty clothing.In addition to the traditional cross-collar
Shangju also had a stand-up collar in the middle and late Ming Dynasty. In addition to inheriting
the pleated skirts and pleated skirts of the previous generation, the skirts also appeared horse-face
skirts.The color of the skirt is light and light, although there are decorations, but not obvious.
In the mid-period, knee laces were often decorated, and skirt laces in the form of embroidery
, gold weaving, and bronzing.In the early years of Chongzhen, most of the skirts were plain white
even with embroidery patterns.A lace is only placed one or two inches below the skirt as a
"presser foot. At the beginning, there were six skirts, which is the so-called "skirt dragging six
Xiangjiang water. Later, I used eight panels. There are many fine pleats around the waist
and the movements are like water patterns.t the end of Ming Dynasty, the decoration of skirts became
A more and more exquisite, and the width of skirts increased to ten.
The pleats at the waist are getting denser.At this time, a skirt appears, each pleat has a color
"When the breeze blows, the color is like moonlight, so it is called "Yuehua skirt
